Got Lift Foils at Camp Cowell in Osage Beach, Missouri

Got Lift Foils was founded in 2021 by Brendan Cowell. Brendan and his family own and operate their business out of Osage Beach, Missouri. They serve customers throughout the Midwest region. Got Lift Foils specializes in eFoil sales, lessons, and demos. Lessons are available for groups, families, and individuals. Although they specialize in eFoils, they also offer lessons and equipment for wing foiling, wake foiling, and dock start foiling.
